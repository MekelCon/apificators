What is SPAMBEE

SPAMBEE is a tool that allows you to easily handle suspicious emails and notify them to experts who will make a complete diagnosis of each transmitted email. Once processed, these emails will feed a database of senders to ban. This "bocklist" will be shared with all SPAMBEE users. In this way the anti-spam filter will be enhanced thanks to the participation of each user.

Why using SPAMBEE

Because we will be able to fight against phishing more efficiently if we fight all together. One bee alone can't do anything, a swarm of bees is powerfull.
